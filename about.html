About Me

I got into graphic design around 2018-2019 when I started to use creative tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, Adobe Animate, and Canva to create images and works, and started to make digital art more. I wanted to pursue a career that involved designing and creating graphics digitally with creative tools, and after research careers like that, I decided that I wanted to get into graphic design. Currently, I am pursuing an advanced diploma in graphic design at Humber Polytechnic.

I am experienced in various different creative tools, including Adobe Creative Suite (Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, After Effects, Premiere Pro, XD), Figma, Webflow, with the ability to adapt to new situations quickly, think logically for the best course of action, and be attentive for any small details.
